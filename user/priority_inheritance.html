The Problem: Priority Inversion

Scenario: Three processes need to run ‚Äî HIGH priority (1), MEDIUM priority (5), and LOW priority (10). LOW acquires a lock that HIGH needs later.

Without priority inheritance: MEDIUM preempts LOW and blocks HIGH indirectly, causing unbounded delays.

With priority inheritance: When HIGH waits, LOW is temporarily boosted to priority 1, allowing it to finish quickly.
